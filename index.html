My Background

When I was a child, I would stuff a bath towel under my door and spend my nights reading Illustrated Classics, Redwall books, and other fiction. By 5th grade, it was decided:
"I will be a writer for a living."
But making a living as a writer isn't easy without education, experience, and skill, so I did the next best thing for someone inclined toward writing and computers did in 2004: I became a marketer. My first job in 2004 was to load data into spreadsheets and early CRMs in an Information Marketing company called The College Network, then later as a Helpdesk Consultant and Technical Writer, then as a Direct Response Copywriter once I learned how to blog and write long-form copy from places like American Writers and Artists (AWAI), Copy Chief with Kevin Rogers (closed as of 2025!), Dan Kennedy's "Magnetic Marketing," and more.
